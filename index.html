About Us

Here is Who we are

Magau Catering can provide you with all your catering needs from corporate deliveries, pickups, luncheons, weddings, and holiday parties. We offers a wide array of rental items from dishes, plates and Cups to lighting, linen, tents and high table makeup. We offer cutting edge design for every event. We serve all of Lake zone from Mwanza, Shinyanga, Mara and Geita regions including Ukerewe Islands. With over 4 years as a catering industry, our experience in catering and event rentals is unmatched.
We are committed to designing and executing flawless, memorable events by combining exquisite food with the highest level of customer service
